Sugar Girl Summary

This melodrama is about a young girl called Rulia who is the only child of her parents. They live in a village enclave far from city centres, being the only child, Rulia parents showered her with much care and love that she finds it difficult to stay a day without the company of her parents. She didn't have to do any house chores as she became lazy.

Rulia is very pretty, intelligent but a lazy girl.

While Rulia parents are industrious farmers having 200 hectares of land for both animal hubandry and crop cultivation. Okafor the father of Rulia is a very strong hunter since his youth while her mother is a devoted and courteous house wife.

The pretty Rulia was sent on an errand by her father to bring some kegs of palm wine to the farm where he will entertain his fellows hunters that came to visits him from the neighboring village. Poor Rulia missed her way due to a singing bird in the bush.
